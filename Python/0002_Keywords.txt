----------------------------------------------- Keywords -----------------------------------------------

- Keywords are the words used in the programming language for it's syntax.
- These words cannot be used for any other purpose then their defined way to use it in the language.

Following is the list of the keywords used in python:

as	def	from	nonlocal	while	assert	del	global	not	with	async	elif	if	or	yield
