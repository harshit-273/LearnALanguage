-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_- Break and Continue -_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-

________________________ Break ________________________

- "break" is used when we want to terminate the loop

________________________ Continue ________________________

- "continue" is used when we want to skip to the next iteration of the loop without proceeding the next part of "continue" in the loop
